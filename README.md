Privacy Policy

BodyCam

Last updated: April 18, 2026


Overview
BodyCam ("the App") is a video recording application designed to help users capture moments using their device camera. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.


Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.
No user accounts

No analytics or tracking
No third-party SDKs

No data is sent to any servers
All data created in the App remains on your device.

Device Permissions
The App requests the following permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

Camera
Used to record videos. Recording only occurs when you explicitly start it. All video is stored locally on your device. We do not access or transmit camera data.

Microphone
Used to capture audio during video recording. Audio is recorded only when you explicitly start recording and is stored locally with your video. We do not access or transmit microphone data.

Photos and Videos / Photo Library
Used only when you choose to save or access media. For example, when you save a recording, it is stored in your device’s media library. The App does not access, scan, or collect your existing photos or videos.
Local Storage

Locations
Used only to provide compass.

All recordings and related content are stored locally on your device. No content leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it using your device’s system sharing features.
Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.
Subscriptions
BodyCam may offer optional paid features or subscriptions. All payments are processed by the platform provider (such as Apple App Store).

We do not have access to your payment details. Payment processing and related data are handled according to the platform provider’s privacy policy.

Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not include accounts, social features, or data collection.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated "Last updated" date.
